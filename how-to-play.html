What's in the Box?

A party trivia game where teams race to uncover hidden phrases, dodge decoys, and unleash wildcards.

🎯 Objective

📦 Guess the Phrase, Win the Box

Each box hides a 3-word phrase related to a category. Teams answer trivia questions to earn turns, then reveal numbered tiles on a grid. Behind each tile is a real word, a decoy, or a wildcard. Pick the right words to complete the phrase and claim the box!

🏆 How to Win

  • There are 5 regular boxes and 1 Super Box.
  • The team that collects the most boxes wins the game.
  • The Super Box is the ultimate prize — harder to crack but worth the glory.
  • Wildcards can steal boxes, freeze opponents, or flip the game on its head.

👥 Who Can Play?

2 to 8 teams — any number of players per team. Perfect for game nights, parties, and classroom fun. No special roles; every team competes equally.

⚙️ Setup

1 Choose Your Teams

Enter 2 to 8 team names on the setup screen. Each team can have any number of players — just huddle together!

2 Understand the Board

The main screen shows 5 regular boxes and a Super Box. Boxes unlock sequentially — you must win the current box before the next one opens. The Super Box unlocks after 2 regular boxes are won.

3 Ready to Go!

Select the first available box to begin. Each box shows its category as a hint (e.g., "Movies", "Space"). The phrase inside is always related to that category.

🎮 Gameplay

Each box follows the same loop until a team completes the phrase.

1. Numerical Question

A trivia question with a numerical answer is shown to all eligible teams. Each team submits their best guess. The team closest to the correct answer wins control of the box for this round.

2. Ordering Question

The winning team must arrange 4 items in the correct order (e.g., chronological, smallest to largest). The number of correct placements determines how many turns they earn.

3. Earn Turns

Turns are based on how well you ordered the items:

Correct PlacementsTurns Earned
4 of 4 correct3 turns
2 of 4 correct2 turns
1 of 4 correct1 turn
0 correct0 — back to step 1

4. Reveal Numbers

Use your earned turns to click numbers on the grid. Each click reveals a real word, a decoy, or a wildcard. See the Grid tab for details.

5. Guess the Phrase

As words appear, try to place them into the 3 blank phrase slots. Correct picks fill a slot. An incorrect pick ends your turn immediately — be careful!

6. Win the Box

Fill all 3 phrase slots correctly and the box is yours! The game returns to the main board for the next box.

💡

If you get 0 correct on the ordering question, you earn no turns and the loop resets to a new numerical question. Another team could steal control!

Prize Fight

Challenge another team. Loser is eliminated from the game!

⚠️

The first box does not contain Steal, Prize Pass, or Prize Fight wildcards — those are replaced with safer wildcards so the game warms up gently.

💎 The Super Box

💎 The Ultimate Challenge

The Super Box unlocks after 2 regular boxes have been won. It's the crown jewel of the game.

  • 2 teams are selected to compete head-to-head.
  • Only those 2 teams participate in numerical and ordering questions.
  • The grid contains 6 decoys instead of the usual 3 — twice the traps!
  • Wildcards are limited to Who's Next, Extra Turn, and Remove Decoy (no Steal, Freeze, or Prize Fight).
  • The loser in a Prize Fight during the Super Box round is eliminated.
